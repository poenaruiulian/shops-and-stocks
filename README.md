# Shops & Stock 
> #### Managing the stock, shops and employees was never easier.

## Description:

> The app provides an easy-to-use interface for shop management, including working with stock, employees, and shops. The app has three types of users: **CEO**, **ShopManager**, and **Employee**, each with specific attributions.
>
> The Employee can modify the shop's stock by adding/removing products from the stock or modifying the quantity of a product. This is the lowest role in importance given the small number of options.
>
> The ShopManager can do all the things an Employee can do and also hire/fire employees for the shop he is assigned to.
>
> The CEO can do everything the other two roles do, but on every shop he owns. Besides, he can hire/fire shop managers and manage the shops he owns. He can close/open a shop and visualize all the shops on his command.
